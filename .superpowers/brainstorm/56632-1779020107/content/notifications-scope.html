Notifications: What kind of alerts do you want?

RunPulse currently has haptic-only HR alerts on the Watch. Let's define the notification strategy.

A

Watch Haptic + Voice

Keep haptic alerts on Watch, add spoken feedback ("Heart rate high, slow down") via Watch speaker/headphones. iOS stays silent during runs.

B

Watch + iOS Push Notifications

Watch gets haptic alerts during runs. iOS also receives a notification when HR threshold is exceeded (useful if phone is nearby but not wearing Watch).

C

Watch Haptic + iOS Voice Report Post-Run

During run: haptic-only on Watch. After run ends: iOS speaks a summary ("You ran 5.2 km in 28 minutes, average heart rate 152 BPM").

D

All of the Above

Full notification suite: haptic + voice during run, iOS push for threshold breaches, post-run voice summary on both devices.
