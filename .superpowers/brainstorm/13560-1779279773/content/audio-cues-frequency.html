Audio Cues — Frequency & Trigger Configuration

When should announcements fire? Pick the trigger model that fits your running style.

A

Distance-Based Only

Announce at every km (or custom interval). Simple, predictable. "Pace: 5:30, HR: 155, Distance: 3 km, Calories: 180"

Default: Every 1 km
Options: 0.5 km, 1 km, 2 km, 5 km
B

Time-Based Only

Announce every X minutes. Good for interval training or time-focused runs.

Default: Every 5 min
Options: 1 min, 5 min, 10 min, 15 min
C

Both (User Chooses)

Configure distance OR time intervals independently. Users pick one or both. Most flexible.

Default: Every 1 km (distance), Time off
Can enable both simultaneously
D

Smart / Adaptive

Announce at km markers + HR threshold warnings + half-distance celebration. Context-aware, not just time/distance.

Triggers: Every km, HR zone changes, 50% goal reached, run complete
